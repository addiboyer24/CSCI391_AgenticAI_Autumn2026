Policies

Collaboration

You are encouraged to discuss assignments in small groups. However, every line of code and every written answer you submit must be your own. Do not share code or written work directly with other students.

Late Work

You have four late days for the semester. Each late day extends a deadline by 24 hours. Late days may not be applied to the final project. Once late days are exhausted, late submissions will not be accepted without prior instructor approval.

Use of AI Tools

Given the subject matter, thoughtful use of AI coding assistants (e.g., GitHub Copilot, Claude) is permitted and encouraged as a learning aid. You must understand and be able to explain every line you submit. Undisclosed wholesale generation of assignment solutions is an honor-code violation.

Submission

All assignments are submitted via Moodle unless stated otherwise. Follow the submission instructions in each handout exactly. Autograded components require correct file naming — re-read instructions before submitting.

Academic Integrity

The University of Montana Student Conduct Code applies to all work in this course. Violations will be referred to the Office of Student Conduct. The standard penalty is course failure or suspension.

Accessibility

Students who require accommodations should contact Disability Services for Students and provide documentation to the instructor within the first two weeks of class.
